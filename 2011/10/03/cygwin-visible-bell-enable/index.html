    Cygwin Visible Bell Enable

    Cygwin is amazing for those of us stuck on Windows. The bell is not always so grand. I prefer a visible bell (like in Putty) rather than a “ding!” when I’ve got my music going.

    To enable the visible bell add the following to ~/.bashrc

    set bell-style visible

    Ah, that’s much better! Thanks again Cygwin!
